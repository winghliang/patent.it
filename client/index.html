About

Patent.It started off as Wing Liang's Coding Dojo project while learning the MEAN stack (MongoDB, ExpressJS, AngularJS, NodeJS). Patent.It is not a real website and does not give nor solicit legal advice. This website is strictly for demonstration purposes.


Patent.It is a website for connecting inventors to attorney/agents who will help the inventors apply for patent applications. In Wing's nearly 10 years as a patent attorney, he has received numerous inquiries from friends and family who believe they have a great idea but don't know the first step in protecting that idea. Law firms typically charge 20-30 thousand dollars to draft a single patent application and for the follow-up with the United States Patent and Trademark Office. Wing created Patent.It as a way for inventors to find experienced attorneys or agents who will do the same work for much less.


Wing has continued to work on the project after graduating from Coding Dojo in order to continue developing as a web developer while learning new things. Things that Wing is currently working on for this website include: (1) validating inputs from both the front end and the backend; (2) creating a fake way for the inventor to pay the attorney/agent for work on a patent application; (3) workflow for attorneys/agents to mark projects as complete; and (4) ways for attorneys/agents to demonstrate that they are qualified for the project they are bidding on.
